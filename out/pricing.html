Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Merios pricing and plans

Can I start with the Free plan and upgrade later?

Yes, absolutely. You can start with our Free plan and upgrade to Pro at any time. Your health data and history will carry over seamlessly, and you'll immediately get access to the full 130+ biomarkers and all 11 health systems.

What biomarkers are included in the Free plan?

The Free plan includes 20 core biomarkers covering cardiovascular health, metabolic function, basic organ function, and nutrient levels. These give you a solid foundation for understanding your health, though the Pro plan provides a much more comprehensive picture.

Is there a commitment required for the Pro plan?

No. Both monthly and annual Pro subscriptions can be cancelled anytime with no penalty. We believe in earning your trust continuously, not locking you in. If you cancel, you retain read-only access to your historical data.

How often should I check my health score?

We recommend monthly check-ins to track meaningful changes. However, you can review your score anytime. With wearable integration, your health data updates continuously, providing real-time insights into trends like sleep quality, activity, and recovery.

Do you offer team or family plans?

Currently, each person needs their own account to maintain data privacy and personalized recommendations. We're exploring family options for a future release. Reach out if this is important to you.
