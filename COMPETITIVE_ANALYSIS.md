# Merios - Competitive Landscape Analysis
**Date: March 2026**

---

## Merios Positioning Recap

Merios is a **native mobile app (iOS)**, available in **French**, that combines:
- **OCR blood test import** (scan with phone camera, 130+ biomarkers)
- **Apple Health / HealthKit integration** (sleep, HR, HRV, activity)
- **Daily subjective journal** (30-second daily check-in)
- **Global health score 0-100** based on **6 pillars** (blood, heart, sleep, activity, nutrition, mental)
- **12 health system analysis** across 13 categories
- **Biological age calculation**
- **Personalized AI insights** and cross-correlations between biomarkers and lifestyle data
- **GDPR-compliant**, EU-hosted data
- **French-first** UX (unique positioning for francophone market)

## Key Insights for Merios Positioning

### 1. Merios has a UNIQUE combination
No single competitor combines ALL of these: OCR blood scan + HealthKit integration + subjective daily journal + unified 0-100 score + biological age + French language + GDPR compliance. **Empirical Health (Health Radar)** is the closest conceptual competitor but lacks the journal pillar, biological age, and French/EU positioning.

### 2. The market is validating the thesis
WHOOP Advanced Labs (Sept 2025), Oura Health Panels (Oct 2025), and Ultrahuman Blood Vision (Oct 2025) all launched blood+wearable integration in late 2025, proving massive demand. But they all require expensive hardware first.

### 3. French/European gap is wide open
**No competitor offers a French-language, GDPR-compliant, mobile-first experience** combining blood tests and wearables. Aware (Germany) is blood-only. The francophone market (France, Belgium, Switzerland, Quebec, West Africa) is underserved.

### 4. OCR blood scan is a rare differentiator
Most competitors either (a) require ordering tests through their platform or (b) offer PDF upload. Merios's **camera-based OCR scanning** of any existing blood test result is a genuine convenience advantage - users can import their existing history instantly.

### 5. The subjective "ressenti" journal is unique
**No competitor** systematically incorporates daily subjective feelings as a core data pillar that feeds into the health score and generates cross-correlations with biomarkers. This is Merios's strongest unique feature.

### 6. Pricing opportunity
Function is $365/yr, InsideTracker $489+/yr, WHOOP $30/mo + $199/test. A **lower-cost, no-hardware-required** approach with OCR import of existing blood tests could capture a large underserved segment.

### 7. Potential partners (not just competitors)
- **Sahha** could be a technology partner (health score API infrastructure)
- **Aware** could be a European lab partner for ordering blood tests
- **Humanity** Age API could enhance biological age calculations
